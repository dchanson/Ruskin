Grace Allen ()

Eldest daughter of George Allen. In 1901, along with her brothers Hugh Allen and William Allen, she became a member of the board of the publishing business now named George Allen and Sons. Grace had an important role in preparing many of her fatherʼs late‐century publications of Ruskinʼs early works. W. G. Collingwood says in Poems ([1891], 1:268) that she transcribed poems for that edition from her fatherʼs copy of the scarce Poems (1850).
See Dawson, People’s Ruskin, 10.

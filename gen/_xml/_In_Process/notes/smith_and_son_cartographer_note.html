Charles Smith & Son

Charles Smith & Son, Cartographers

The manufacturer of Ruskin's celestial globe (17 cm x 88.9 mm, object GA2197, Southwark Heritage Centre and Walworth Library, London). Charles Smith (1768-1854) was appointed map seller to the Prince of Wales in 1809. The firm traded under the name Smith & Son from 1826 to 1854, when it was taken over by Smith's son, William, and later his grandson, Guildford.

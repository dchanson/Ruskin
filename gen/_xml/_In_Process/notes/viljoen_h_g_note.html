Helen Gill Viljoen
In May through 30 July 1977, an exhibition at the Pierpont Morgan celebrated Viljoenʼs bequest to the library. No catalog was issued, but a notice in the Ruskin Newsletter (16 [spring 1977]: 1) mentions that, of items associated with the childhood, “Ruskinʼs toy soldiers” were included.

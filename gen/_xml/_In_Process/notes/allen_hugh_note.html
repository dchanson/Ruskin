Hugh Allen ()

Next eldest son, after his brother William, of George Allen. In 1901, along with William and his sister Grace, he became a member of the board of the publishing business now named George Allen and Sons. With E. T. Cook, he cataloged the drawings for the bibliography volume (vol. 38) of the Library Edition. Hugh served as an agent for at the important Sothebyʼs sales of 1930 and 1931 of Ruskinʼs remaining library and manuscripts.
See Dawson, People’s Ruskin, 10; Dearden, “Library Edition of the Works of John Ruskin”,ref> 61.

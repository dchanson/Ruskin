Missing name (MS VIII)—Evidently undecided about a name for the castle, Ruskin left a gap for the name to be filled later. His eventual decision, as printed in Friendship's Offering, was Castel Alto.


“the old man” (MS VIII)—In the final text for Friendship's Offering, Ruskin replaced "the old man" with the name he had since settled, Amalfiero.

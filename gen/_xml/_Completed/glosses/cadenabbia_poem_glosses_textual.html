Pause–period (MS VIII)—See Editorial and Encoding Rationale and Methodology: Element, Attribute, and Value Usage—Commas, Periods, and Other Punctuation.


“And quick as sun‐red waves” (MS VIII; Poems [1891]; Works [1903])—Ruskin initially wrote “sunwaves” as a single word, and then he placed a caret between “sun” and “waves” and inserted above the line “‐red” to form “sun‐red waves”. Collingwood mistranscribed the insertion as “sunned”, and the editors of the Library Edition adopted that reading.

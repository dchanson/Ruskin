Terminal comma (MS VIII)—See Editorial and Encoding Rationale and Methodology: Element, Attribute, and Value Usage—Commas, Periods, and Other Punctuation.


“traditionary” (MS VIII; Works [1903])—The appearance of this word, which in manuscript is divided after the first syllable on the line break, without punctuation by a hyphen to indicate word division, misled the editors of the Library Edition to misread the modifier as “true dictionary“.
